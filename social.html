Oak Summit: Free and Public Online Event
Saturday, March 20, 2021
11 AM EDT to 2 PM EDT

The Oak Summit will be held on Zoom for individuals wishing to gather in solidarity on behalf of the Oaks. Guest speakers will address various topics including, the planned felling of over 1,500 centenarian Oaks to rebuild the spire at Notre Dame Cathedral in France and the San Carlos Apache struggle at Oak Flat in Arizona.
